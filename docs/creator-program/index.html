Creator program

Unlock promotion slots, analytics, and funding for your game by partnering with Gurjot's Games.

Apply now ↗

Program perks

  • Spotlight placement

    Guaranteed rotation in the Discover → Editor’s spotlight mega menu.

  • Revenue share

    70/30 split on in-game purchases processed through Gurjot Pay.

  • Launch funding

    Eligible teams can receive microgrants for art, QA, or localization support.

  • Insight dashboard

    Early access to cohort analytics, retention curves, and controller usage heatmaps.

Requirements

  1. 1. Active development

    Your title should have at least one playable chapter or mode updated within the last 90 days.

  2. 2. Dedicated support channel

    Maintain a support email or Discord channel so players can report issues.

  3. 3. Community guidelines

    Agree to the community code of conduct and moderation expectations.

How to apply

Email creators@gurjotsgames.com with the following:

  • Game slug and public trailer or gameplay video.
  • Team size and primary contact.
  • Monetization plans (if any) and regional availability.

We reply within two weeks with next steps, including build review and contract paperwork if approved.

Already a partner? Access the portal via the launcher or email creators@gurjotsgames.com for help.
